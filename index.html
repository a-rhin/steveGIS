Services

GIS Analysis & Mapping

I provide spatial data analysis and cartographic solutions for environmental, urban, and resource studies. My work involves data cleaning, spatial modeling, and map creation using tools like ArcGIS Pro, ArcMap, ENVI and Google Earth Engine to uncover meaningful spatial patterns and trends.

Remote Sensing & Image Interpretation

I process and analyze satellite imagery to detect land cover changes, monitor environmental conditions, and support spatial decision-making. My experience includes using Landsat imagery, vegetation indices, and supervised classification techniques to generate accurate spatial insights.

Research & Spatial Reporting

I support research that requires geospatial input — from data acquisition and analysis to visualization and interpretation. My work contributes to studies in urbanization, environmental management, flooding, land surface temperature, shoreline change, Vegetation Health Index and land cover change.
